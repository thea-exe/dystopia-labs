How It Works

TouchGold connects convenience and predictability for those who can't

  • Payment channel technology allows infinite transactions between two individuals while only incurring gas fees for opening the channel.
  • Practically free and superspeed transactions are achieved using digitally signed and hash-locked transfers, called balance proofs, fully collateralized by previously setup on-chain deposits.
  • Network protocol removes barriers with single-target limitations of transactions, and enables transactions between individuals not immediately connected by routing and interlocking channel transfers.
  • Once a channel is created, participants may issue what can be considered certified checks freely back and forth. Instead of keeping track of all checks, however, each peer only keeps a copy of the latest one.

The Major Problem

The blockchain industry is gradually rising to become one of the biggest emerging technologies in the world. With a fast paced environment, the financial industry is without a doubt, going to be disrupted.

Public Unfamiliarity with ETH
  • Blockchain is an emerging technology, meaning it's on the rise to success, but hasn't made its' name known
No Existing Service on L2 Protocol
  • Current payment methods on L1 chain is inefficient: pay $50 to transfer $50
  • No way to circumvent the high gas fees except for setting your own gas limit or just paying the fee
Bank Hesitancy
  • Potential illegal activity can be on the blockchain due to limitless transfers
  • No profit incentives as banks would need to investigate the owner and their source of funds from any illegal activity
  • No lack of regulation means that banks can't find out where the source of money is coming from, legal or not
No Public Stablecoin Exchange
  • No method of exchanging public StableCoins backed by governments (e.g. there is no USDC + EUR-L pair)
